    • A LOOK AT THE ADS ASTRONOMY LITERATURE DATABASE

    Objective: Using the SAO/NASA Astrophysics Data System, build a predictive model of the citation count of an article published in one of the main astrophysics journal.
    The ADS corpus consists of 10 million bibliographic records, over 70 million citations, and 3.6 million full-text documents, but we will restrict ourselves to articles published in the Astrophysical Journal,Astronomy and Astrophysics, Publications of the Astronomical Society of Japan, Monthly Notices of the Royal Astronomical Society, the Astronomical journal,Icarus, Nature and Science, between 1990 and 2014 included.

    Tools: pandas, SQLite, webscraping, BeautifulSoup, and more

    • YAHOO FINANCE DATABASE

    Objective: Create a framework to analyze simple price data from the yahoo finance database
    A few features for each symbol listed in NYSE, NASDAQ, AMEX are downloaded into a SQLite database, accessible through python. Retrieval and manipulation of data is done through a class containing methods to analyze pricing time series.
